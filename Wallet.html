About the Kroon Digital Voucher Wallet

The Kroon Digital Voucher Wallet is an innovative solution designed to address financial inclusion challenges and stimulate local economic activity in Ocean View, South Africa. As part of the iNethi community network initiative, this digital wallet enables residents to easily manage, send, and receive community vouchers, fostering a more resilient local economy.

Developed through a co-design process with the Ocean View community, the Kroon wallet aims to overcome barriers to financial inclusion, keep resources circulating within the community, and support the growth of local businesses and services. By providing a secure and user-friendly platform for digital transactions, the Kroon wallet empowers residents to participate more actively in the local economy, even in areas with limited internet connectivity.

Key Features

  • User-friendly interface designed through community co-design workshops
  • Real-time balance tracking with clear visual indicators
  • Secure transactions using PIN protection
  • Offline capability for use without constant internet connection
  • Integration with other iNethi services
  • Support for local business transactions and community service exchanges
  • Reward system for community network contributors

Impact on Ocean View

The Kroon Digital Voucher Wallet is more than just a payment system â€“ it's a tool for community building and digital inclusion. By providing an accessible, community-focused alternative to traditional financial services, the Kroon wallet is helping to create a more resilient and interconnected Ocean View.

Through the Kroon system:

  • Community members who contribute to the iNethi network, such as Wi-Fi antenna hosts, are rewarded with Kroon tokens.
  • These tokens can be exchanged for internet services or other locally curated content, creating a sustainable ecosystem.
  • Local businesses can accept Kroon as payment, encouraging local trade and economic growth.
  • Residents without traditional bank accounts can participate in the digital economy.
  • The community gains more control over its economic resources, reducing dependency on external financial systems.

By keeping resources circulating within Ocean View, the Kroon system helps build a more self-reliant and vibrant local economy, while also incentivizing community engagement with the iNethi network.

How It Works

  1. Download the iNethi app and create a Kroon wallet account.
  2. Earn Kroon tokens by contributing to the community network or through local exchanges.
  3. Use the wallet to send or receive Kroon for goods and services within the community.
  4. Track your balance and transaction history in real-time.
  5. Enjoy secure, fast, and low-cost transactions, even without an internet connection.
